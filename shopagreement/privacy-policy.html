Zero Data Collection

BuyLedgerH does not require an account, subscription, in-app purchase, or paid unlock. Purchase records are stored locally on the device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how BuyLedgerH handles privacy when you use the BuyLedgerH mobile application. We believe privacy should be clear, respectful, and easy to understand. Because BuyLedgerH does not collect user information, this policy is designed to make that position transparent.

2. Information We Collect

BuyLedgerH does not collect any personal information, non-personal information, device identifiers, location data, contact information, photos, files, analytics data, crash logs, advertising identifiers, or behavioral usage data from users.

  • No account registration is required.
  • No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact lists are collected.
  • No precise or approximate location data is collected.
  • No analytics, advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking is used.
  • No user content is uploaded to servers operated by BuyLedgerH.

3. How We Use Information

Since BuyLedgerH does not collect user information, we do not use user information for any purpose. We do not analyze, process, profile, sell, rent, disclose, or monetize personal information.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

BuyLedgerH does not share user information with third parties because no user information is collected. We do not sell personal data, disclose personal data to advertisers, or transfer user information to external analytics providers.

5. Data Storage and Security

BuyLedgerH does not maintain servers, databases, or cloud storage systems containing user information. Because no user information is collected by BuyLedgerH, there is no personal data stored by us to secure, retain, or delete.

In short: BuyLedgerH is built so that your purchase records stay with you.

6. Children's Privacy

BuyLedgerH does not knowingly collect information from children or from any other users. If you believe that any information has been provided to us by mistake, please contact us so we can review the matter.

7. Third-Party Services

BuyLedgerH does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools that collect user information through the app. If the app is distributed through an app store, the store operator may process information under its own privacy policy. Such processing is controlled by the app store provider, not by BuyLedgerH.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, privacy laws may provide rights related to access, deletion, correction, portability, restriction, objection, or withdrawal of consent. Because BuyLedgerH does not collect user information, we generally do not hold personal data that can be accessed, corrected, or deleted. You may still contact us with any privacy-related question.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect app changes, legal requirements, or operational updates. If we make changes, we will update the effective date shown at the top of this policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or BuyLedgerH's privacy practices, please contact us at buyledger@example.com.
